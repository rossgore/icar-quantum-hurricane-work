PHASE 5 SUMMARY — Quantum Feature Engineering for Hurricane RI
======================================================================

Dataset:          HURDAT2 Atlantic 1980–2023, 123 storms
Labelable obs:    2,506  (wind >= 35 kt, 24-h window complete)
RI events:        143 (5.7%)  |  Class imbalance: 1:17
Quantum circuit:  6 qubit(s), 32 Pauli observables
Augmented vector: 6 classical + 32 shadow = 38 total features

BOOTSTRAP RESULTS (n=1,000, 95% CI):
  Best baseline AUC-ROC:  0.7539
  Best quantum AUC-ROC:   0.7550  (Δ = +0.0011)

  Logistic Regression (strongest quantum gain):
    Baseline  AUC=0.7351 [0.6284, 0.8259]
    Quantum   AUC=0.7550 [0.6734, 0.8313]
    ΔAUC = +0.0199

OPERATIONAL METRICS (optimal CSI threshold):
  Best baseline CSI:  0.1724
  Best quantum CSI:   0.1429  (Δ = -0.0296)
